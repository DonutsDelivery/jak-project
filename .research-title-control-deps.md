## TL;DR for the dispatcher

For the REPL spawn call itself to even *compile*, the jakx `title-obs.gc` must export the symbols `title-control`, `title-control-init`, and `*title-control*`. Right now it exports NONE of them — the 3-line stub means the REPL expression won't even compile, because `title-control` (the type) and `title-control-init` (the behavior) are unknown. So **step zero** is to populate jakx's `levels/title/title-obs.gc` with at least the `title-control` deftype + a stub `title-control-init` behavior. Once the REPL accepts the call, we iterate on runtime errors.
